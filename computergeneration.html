Computer Generation

First Generation of Computer

Vacuum Tube

Vacuum tubes have the functionality of controlling the flow of electronics in a vacuum. Generally, it is used in switches, amplifiers, radios, televisions, etc.


Second Generation of Computer

Transistor

A transistor helps in controlling the flow of electricity in devices, it works as an amplifier or a switch.


Third Generation of Computer

Integrated Circuit

are silicon chips that contain their circuit elements like transistors, resistors, etc.


Fourth Generation of Computer

Microprocessors

are the components that contain the CPU and its circuits and are present in the Integrated Circuit.


Fifth Generation of Computer

Artificial Intelligence

deals with creating intelligent machines and behaviors.
